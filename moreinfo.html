Data Analytics Skills

  • Collect, process, analyze, and present data to best support a client's needs, from supporting everyday business decisions to driving global change.
  • To help others better understand the insights data can provide by making the data more accessible and easy to understand.
  • Preparing and cleaning data for analysis by filtering, removing duplicate values, filling in missing data, and standardizing data.
  • Data exploration and analysis by statistical techniques to explore and analyze data, identify patterns, and see other connections throughout the data.
  • Data visualizations that highlight interesting data points through graphs, charts, images, and dashboards to make the data more understandable.
  • Reporting the data to the client via presentations to communicate the insights and other findings from the data to help influence policy and decision making.
  • Collaboration with other departments or groups to understand their data or insight needs to help make more informed decisions based on previous data insights.
  • Identify and work through process shortcomings and optimize workflows to avoid unnecessary bottlenecks and ensure projects are completed on time.

Importance of Data Analytics

  • Improved efficiency by reducing expenses, improving productivity, and streamlining processes and operations.
  • Understanding risk management by identifying potential challenges or risks allows businesses to develop strategies to mitigate identified risks.
  • Improve customer experiences by showcasing customer preferences and behaviors, allowing businesses to develop improved products, services, and facilities.
  • Improved decision making for companies by gaining insights that can assist business strategies moving forward.

Quality Assurance Skills

  • Develop custom test plans to ensure all requirements are met or exceeded.
  • Write very detailed reports on each issue discovered during testing process.
  • Deploy tests across multiple devices to discover network related issues.
  • Perform multiple tests with several different elements to pinpoint potential causes.
